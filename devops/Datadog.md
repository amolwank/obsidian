Datadog is a cloud-based monitoring and observability tool used by DevOps, SREs, developers and IT Teams to track the health, performance and security of application and infrastructure in real time.

What Datadog does?
- Monitoring and Observability: Collects metrics, logs and traces from servers, databases, containers, cloud services, and applications.
- Virtualization: Provide dashboards and graphs to visualize system performance.
- Alerting: Sends alerts when threasholds are breached (e.g. CPU usage too high).
- Correlation: Helps connect metrics, logs, and traces to thoubleshoot issues faster.
- Security: Detects anomalies and potential security threats in infrastructure.
